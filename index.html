    Here is a timeline of Alan Turing's life:

  • 1936 - Created concept of Turing Machine
  • 1939-1942 - Cracked U-boat enigma code, turned tides of Battle of Atlantic
  • 1946 - One of first in the world to begin computer hardware and software work
  • 1947 and 1948 - Concepts of neural networks and artifical intelligence
  • 1954 - Suicide by Cyanide poisoning after years of strife with government over homosexuality
